About This Data

What is The Capital Index?

The Capital Index is a free, open-access accountability tool that aggregates publicly available data on all 538 members of the 119th United States Congress. It is built and maintained by The Founders Brief and is not affiliated with any government agency.

Data Sources & Methodology

Congress.gov API
Member profiles, sponsored legislation, committee assignments, and term data. Updated daily by the Library of Congress. api.congress.gov ↗
Federal Election Commission (FEC)
Campaign finance totals, donor breakdowns, and disbursements. FEC candidate matching uses last name + chamber + state. Some members may not match if name formats differ. fec.gov/data ↗
GovTrack.us
Voting statistics including party-line vote percentage, missed votes, ideology scores, and leadership scores. Ideology scores range from −1 (most progressive) to +1 (most conservative) based on voting patterns, not self-identification. GovTrack methodology ↗
Capitol Trades / 2iQ Research
STOCK Act trade disclosures. No public API is available; the site links directly to each member's Capitol Trades profile. capitoltrades.com ↗
unitedstates/congress-legislators
Social media handles (Twitter/X, YouTube, Facebook) for members of Congress, maintained by the open-source @unitedstates project. GitHub ↗
U.S. Census Bureau Geocoder
ZIP code to congressional district lookup for the "My Reps" feature. census.gov ↗

Limitations

All data is sourced from public government APIs and third-party aggregators. FEC candidate matching is best-effort and may occasionally link to the wrong candidate for members with common names. GovTrack ideology scores reflect voting patterns only and should not be taken as a definitive political characterization. Stock trade data is self-reported by members and may be delayed up to 45 days by law.

Built By

The Capital Index is a project of The Founders Brief, an independent civic technology publication. We are not affiliated with any political party, government agency, or advocacy organization.
